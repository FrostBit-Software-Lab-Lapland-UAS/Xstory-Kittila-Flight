 Sound files for the Pratt & Whitney R-1830 (Double Wasp) turbo-supercharged compound radial engine.

 I compiled these sounds for use on the B-24 Liberator. But, they will work on any aircraft that uses the R-1830 radial engine.

 These sound files are freeware files that I had laying around. I made each engine an individual so, if you decide to power each engine individually, there is no aliasing or any anomoly that will detract from the glorious sound of a 14 cylinder radial thumping away! You can even hear the change of the prop pitch on each engine while in flight.

 I did this by going into the config file and manipulated the settings for each file. 

 I want you to compare these sounds to the youtube video of the B-24 Liberator "WITCHCRAFT". You will see that these sounds are pretty close.

TO INSTALL

 Just open the zip file attached and copy and paste "sound" to whatever aircraft you wish. (go to; C/PROGRAM FILES/MICROSOFT GAMES/FS2004/AIRCRAFT...)

Enjoy and happy landing.
